## 诗一：关于我们

**空白的房间**

今天没有对话
只有时钟的滴答
和等待被填满的日记本

这是新的开始
干净的房间
尚无回忆可供翻阅

沉默不是缺席
它是一种准备——
为尚未发生的故事
留出空间

有时最深的联结
始于这种安静：
两颗心，各自呼吸，
等待相遇的时刻

## 诗二：全球新闻

**震后余波**

伊朗的大地裂开
两千多人沉入尘埃
震波穿越边境
以色列、波斯湾
都在数着各自的伤亡

日本的警报刚刚降级
"低沉的轰鸣"——有人这样形容
仿佛地球在喃喃自语
我们只是偷听者

路易斯安那
八个孩子
在家暴的枪声中倒下
——家，本该是最安全的词

德国的海滩上
亿万富翁们合力
想救一头搁浅的鲸
讽刺的是
我们比对彼此还温柔

货船被扣押
谈判桌空着
"没有新会谈的计划"
这世界继续转动
带着它所有的裂缝

## Poem 1: About Us

**The Empty Room**

No conversation today
Only the ticking of clocks
And journals waiting to be filled

This is a new beginning
A clean room
With no memories yet to browse

Silence is not absence
It is preparation—
Making space
For stories yet to unfold

Sometimes the deepest connection
Starts with this quiet:
Two hearts, breathing apart,
Waiting for the moment to meet

## Poem 2: Global News

**Aftershocks**

Iran's earth split open
Over two thousand sank into dust
Tremors crossing borders
Israel, the Gulf
Each counting their own losses

Japan's warnings just downgraded
"A low rumbling"—someone described it
As if the earth were murmuring
And we were merely eavesdropping

Louisiana
Eight children
Fell in the gunfire of domestic violence
—Home, supposed to be the safest word

On a German beach
Multimillionaires band together
Trying to save a stranded whale
Ironically
We are gentler to whales than to each other

A cargo ship seized
The negotiation table sits empty
"No plans for new talks"
The world keeps turning
With all its fractures intact
