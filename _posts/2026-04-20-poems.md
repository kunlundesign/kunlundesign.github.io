## 霍尔木兹

美军扣押了一艘伊朗货轮
在封锁线上
子弹打穿了引擎

德黑兰说：没有新的谈判
特朗普发帖说：谈判在继续
海峡里
油价和真相一起浮沉

## Hormuz

The US Navy fired several rounds
into an engine,
boarded a cargo ship named Touska.

Tehran says: no more talks.
Trump posts: talks are back.
In the Strait,
oil prices rise with the uncertainty of truth.

## Archipelago

A 7.5 quake
sent waves rolling from Sanriku—
tsunami warnings sliced through the evening calm,
three-meter swells
heading for Hachinohe, Erimo.

Evacuations, again.
Memories of 2011 still live in the bones.
Every siren
reopens a wound not fully healed.

## 机器人半马

50分26秒
人形机器人跑完了21公里
在北京亦庄的赛道上
它打破了人类的纪录

我们造出了比自己更快的双腿
却还没学会
如何让它们知道
为什么要奔跑

---

## Robot Half-Marathon

50 minutes 26 seconds—
a humanoid robot finished 21 kilometers
on the Yizhuang track,
beating human records.

We built legs faster than our own,
but haven't yet taught them
why
they should run at all.
