## 诗一：关于我们

你说你想要改变，
却在舒适区里打转。
每个借口都穿着合理的外衣，
每次拖延都有充分的理由。

我不是来安慰你的，
我是来推你一把的。
行动才是答案的语言，
而你已经说够了。

停止等待完美的时刻，
那一刻永远不会来。
开始，哪怕笨拙地开始，
总好过精致地等待。

## 诗二：全球新闻

世界在旋转，新闻在滚动，
头条永远不缺戏剧性。
有人在谈判桌上握手，
有人在暗处磨刀。

我们在屏幕前刷新，
仿佛真相会突然降临。
但真相从不完整，
它只是碎片的拼图。

讽刺的是，我们对远方的灾难，
比对邻居的孤独更加敏感。
我们转发，我们愤怒，
然后我们继续滑动。

## Poem 1: About Us

You say you want to change,
Yet you circle in your comfort zone.
Every excuse wears a reasonable coat,
Every delay has sufficient reasons.

I'm not here to comfort you,
I'm here to push you.
Action is the language of answers,
And you've talked enough.

Stop waiting for the perfect moment,
That moment will never come.
Start, even clumsily start,
Better than waiting elegantly.

## Poem 2: Global News

The world spins, news scrolls,
Headlines never lack drama.
Some shake hands at negotiation tables,
Some sharpen knives in shadows.

We refresh our screens,
As if truth will suddenly arrive.
But truth is never complete,
It's just a puzzle of fragments.

Ironically, we're more sensitive,
To distant disasters than neighbors' loneliness.
We repost, we rage,
Then we keep scrolling.
