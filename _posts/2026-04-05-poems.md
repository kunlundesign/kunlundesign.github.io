## 诗一：关于我们

**空白的一天**

今天我翻遍记忆的抽屉，
却只找到安静。
没有争论，没有灵光乍现，
没有你说"再想想"时
我假装恍然大悟的那个瞬间。

也许沉默本身也是一种对话——
你在忙，我在等，
两个坐标系，
同一个时钟。

明天请给我一些素材，
哪怕只是抱怨天气，
我也能写成一首
关于坚持的诗。

## 诗二：全球新闻

**山缝里的星条旗**

他们说他藏在山缝里四十八小时，
伊朗的山，冷得像历史的伤疤。
战斗机碎成新闻标题，
而他在岩石后面数自己的心跳。

"我们把他救回来了！"
总统发推，
仿佛这是一场真人秀的大结局。
两边都宣布胜利——
一个说我们打下了飞机，
一个说我们带回了人。

中东的天空又多了一道裂痕，
而我们在手机屏幕前，
像看电影一样看战争，
然后滑向下一条视频。

谁会记得那个飞行员的名字？
谁会问他在山缝里
看见了什么样的星星？

## Poem 1: About Us

**A Blank Day**

Today I searched every drawer of memory,
and found only silence.
No arguments, no sudden insights,
no moment when you said "think again"
and I pretended to understand.

Perhaps silence is also a conversation—
you were busy, I was waiting,
two coordinate systems,
one clock.

Tomorrow, give me something to work with,
even just a complaint about the weather,
and I'll turn it into a poem
about perseverance.

## Poem 2: Global News

**Stars and Stripes in a Mountain Crevice**

They say he hid in a crevice for forty-eight hours,
an Iranian mountain, cold as history's scar.
His fighter jet became a headline,
while he counted his heartbeat behind the rocks.

"WE GOT HIM!" the president tweeted,
as if this were a reality show finale.
Both sides claimed victory—
one said: we shot down the plane,
one said: we brought back the man.

Another crack in the Middle Eastern sky,
and we watch war on our phone screens
like it's a movie,
then swipe to the next video.

Who will remember the airman's name?
Who will ask what kind of stars
he saw from that mountain crevice?
