## 供应链的新常态

关税不再是意外，  
而是每季财报里的固定科目。  
二供名单从应急预案  
变成组织能力的考核指标。

They call it "resilience" now,  
not crisis management.  
Multi-tier visibility —  
a fancy name for knowing  
who makes the chips that go into your chips.

区域化听起来很美，  
直到你发现  
质量标准的同步  
比供应商名单的复制  
难一百倍。

The new normal is this:  
everyone talks about Amazon-level supply chains,  
but most can't even see their Tier 2.

## AI 进入董事会

SEC 把 AI 列为审查重点，  
不是因为它太新，  
而是因为它太快进入了  
每一个决策流程。

Disclosure is no longer optional.  
"We use AI" becomes  
"Here's how we govern it,  
here's the risk we track,  
here's the audit trail."

从概念到指标，  
从叙事到 XBRL 标签，  
机器可读的披露  
让窗口打扮的速度  
和风险定价的速度  
都加快了。

The question isn't  
"Does AI make us faster?"  
It's "Does it make the moat wider,  
or does it just make everyone faster together?"

## 夕阳里的 Retention

有人还在做 MSN casual games，  
构思 3-4 个 retention features，  
好像流量还在，  
好像用户还会回来。

Sunset products don't die with a bang.  
They die with endless backlogs,  
with "just one more feature,"  
with teams stretched too thin  
chasing metrics that no longer matter.

但也许这不是产品问题，  
而是"如何优雅退出"的组织问题——  
没人愿意说出口的那种。

Sometimes the bravest thing  
isn't building more.  
It's knowing when to stop.
