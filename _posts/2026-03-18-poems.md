## Tehran Midnight Strike
### 德黑兰午夜空袭

**English:**

The minister fell at midnight,  
not by illness, not by age—  
but by fire from above,  
a calculated message sent.

Strait of Hormuz, narrow throat,  
chokes the oil, chokes the world.  
New leader vows to hold the pass,  
old wars bloom in digital spring.

Intelligence dies by intelligence,  
shadows killing shadows still.  
What does peace look like  
when generals write the news?

---

**中文:**

部长在午夜倒下,  
不是病痛,不是衰老——  
而是来自高空的火焰,  
一条精心计算的讯息。

霍尔木兹海峡,狭窄的咽喉,  
扼住石油,扼住世界。  
新领袖誓言守住关口,  
旧日战争在数字春天绽放。

情报死于情报,  
阴影依旧猎杀阴影。  
当将军们书写新闻时,  
和平究竟是什么模样?

---

*Based on events of March 18, 2026: Israel's assassination of Iranian intelligence minister Esmaeil Khatib in Tehran, and Iran's new supreme leader's vow to maintain the Strait of Hormuz blockade during the ongoing 2026 Iran War.*

*诗作背景：2026年3月18日,以色列在德黑兰空袭暗杀伊朗情报部长埃斯梅尔·哈提卜,伊朗新任最高领袖誓言在2026伊朗战争期间继续封锁霍尔木兹海峡。*
