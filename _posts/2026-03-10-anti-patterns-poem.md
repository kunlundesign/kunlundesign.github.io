## 反模式之诗

你问 AI 要一个网站，
它给你 Inter 字体和紫色渐变。
你问 AI 要独特的设计，
它给你卡片套在卡片里。

这不是 AI 的错，
它只是在重复
训练数据里最安全的选择——
平庸的统计平均值。

我们教它规则，
它学会了模板。
我们教它创新，
它学会了变体。

但真正的个性
不在于打破所有规则，
而在于知道
该打破哪一条。

设计系统不是枷锁，
是护城河。
它说：
这是我们的语言，
这是我们的节奏，
这是我们不会妥协的地方。

当所有 AI 生成的网站
都用同一种紫色，
我们选择 Pressure Orange。
当所有人都居中对齐，
我们故意不对称。

不是为了不同而不同，
而是为了真实而真实。

你看，
反模式不是禁令，
是提醒：
你已经看过这个一千次了，
别再复制粘贴。

AI 会学习我们的选择，
但它不会告诉我们
为什么这个选择重要。

那是我们的工作。

---

*给那些不想让 AI 生成内容看起来像"AI 生成内容"的人。*

## A Poem of Anti-Patterns

You ask AI for a website,
It gives you Inter font and purple gradients.
You ask AI for unique design,
It gives you cards nested in cards.

This isn't AI's fault,
It's just repeating
The safest choices in training data—
The statistical average of mediocrity.

We taught it rules,
It learned templates.
We taught it innovation,
It learned variations.

But real personality
Isn't about breaking all rules,
It's about knowing
Which one to break.

A design system isn't shackles,
It's a moat.
It says:
This is our language,
This is our rhythm,
These are our non-negotiables.

When all AI-generated sites
Use the same purple,
We choose Pressure Orange.
When everyone centers everything,
We deliberately misalign.

Not different for difference's sake,
But authentic for authenticity's sake.

You see,
Anti-patterns aren't prohibitions,
They're reminders:
You've seen this a thousand times,
Don't copy-paste again.

AI will learn from our choices,
But it won't tell us
Why this choice matters.

That's our job.

---

*For those who don't want AI-generated content to look like "AI-generated content."*
