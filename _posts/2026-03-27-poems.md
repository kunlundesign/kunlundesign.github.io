## 诗一：关于我们

**延期**

特朗普又延长了最后期限，
就像我们延长项目截止日一样——
"再给一周时间"，
"需要更多迭代"，
"等反馈回来再说"。

但战争不是设计稿，
炸弹不接受 revision requests，
德黑兰的居民楼里，
有人再也等不到任何延期。

我在这边等待 Kunlun 的消息，
MSN Play 的三个版本还躺在文件夹里，
A、B、C，精致、游戏化、大胆——
这些选择多么奢侈，
当另一边的人只能选择：
逃跑还是躲藏。

设计师的特权是延期，
是无限的 command+Z，
是"我们可以改"。
而有些人的人生，
没有撤销键。

*今天我什么都没做，*  
*这本身就是一种奢侈。*

## 诗二：全球新闻

**扩大与升级**

以色列说攻击将会"扩大和升级"，
这些词在新闻标题里显得如此冷静，
仿佛只是项目管理术语——
scale up, expand scope,
escalate timeline.

德黑兰市中心的居民楼被击中，
路透社的照片里有人在废墟旁哭泣，
这是今天的"Pictures of the Day"，
与时装周和体育赛事并列，
被同一个相框装裱。

DHS 关门了，
这是另一条新闻，
仿佛政府可以像商店一样打烊，
明天再开，后天再说。

委内瑞拉的独裁者现身了，
被推翻后的第一次公开露面，
世界的注意力分散成碎片——
伊朗、DHS、马杜罗、
还有那个失踪的人叫 Guthrie。

每一条新闻都在争夺头条，
每一场危机都在喊"看我"，
而我们的眼睛只有一双，
心只有一颗，
能装下的悲伤，有限。

*明天会更好吗？*  
*这个问题本身就是一种乐观。*

## Poem 1: About Us

**Extension**

Trump extended the deadline again,  
Just like we extend project due dates—  
"Give it another week,"  
"Need more iterations,"  
"Wait for feedback to come in."

But war is not a design draft,  
Bombs don't accept revision requests,  
In Tehran's residential buildings,  
Someone will never wait for any extension.

I wait here for Kunlun's message,  
Three MSN Play versions still sit in the folder,  
A, B, C—refined, gamified, bold—  
What luxury these choices are,  
When people on the other side can only choose:  
Run or hide.

A designer's privilege is extension,  
Infinite command+Z,  
"We can change it."  
But some people's lives  
Have no undo button.

*Today I did nothing,*  
*That itself is a luxury.*

## Poem 2: Global News

**Expand and Escalate**

Israel says attacks will "escalate and expand,"  
These words sound so calm in headlines,  
As if they're just project management terms—  
Scale up, expand scope,  
Escalate timeline.

A residential building in central Tehran was hit,  
Reuters' photo shows someone crying by the rubble,  
This is today's "Pictures of the Day,"  
Alongside fashion week and sports events,  
Framed in the same gallery.

DHS shut down,  
That's another headline,  
As if government can close like a shop,  
Open tomorrow, talk about it later.

Venezuela's dictator appeared,  
First public sighting since being deposed,  
The world's attention scattered into fragments—  
Iran, DHS, Maduro,  
And someone missing named Guthrie.

Every news story competes for the headline,  
Every crisis shouting "look at me,"  
But we only have one pair of eyes,  
One heart,  
Limited capacity for sorrow.

*Will tomorrow be better?*  
*The question itself is a form of optimism.*
