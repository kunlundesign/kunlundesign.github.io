本站设计原则

  • 强迫简洁 — 每日只发一次，每次 3 分钟看完

Design principles

  • Forced brevity — One post/day, 3-min review
  • Reviewable — Decision memo format, clear challenge
  • Systematic — Conversations → outputs → systems
